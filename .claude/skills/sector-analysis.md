# Sector Analysis Skill

Provide deep expertise on business processes for a specific industry sector.

## Available Sectors

### E-commerce / DTC
Key processes: Order fulfillment, Returns, Supplier management, Customer support, Marketing campaigns, Billing
Critical metrics: Order-to-ship time, Return rate, Stockout rate, CSAT, ROAS
Common bottlenecks: Manual order validation, slow supplier response, delayed tracking notifications

### SaaS / Tech
Key processes: Client onboarding, Bug resolution, Feature development, Customer success
Critical metrics: Time to value, Activation rate, Bug resolution time, Churn rate
Common bottlenecks: Slow handoff from Sales to CS, bugs not deployed to production, long onboarding

### Consulting / Agency
Key processes: Project delivery, Client reporting, Time tracking, Billing
Critical metrics: Project margin, Deadline adherence, Client retention, Utilization rate
Common bottlenecks: Scope creep, late timesheets, slow client approvals, report delays

### Real Estate
Key processes: Lead to close, Tenant management, Property maintenance
Critical metrics: Lead response time, Conversion rate, Occupancy rate, Maintenance response
Common bottlenecks: Slow lead follow-up, lengthy closing process, maintenance backlogs

### Healthcare / Wellness
Key processes: Patient journey, Supplement/product fulfillment, Compliance
Critical metrics: Wait time, No-show rate, Fulfillment time, Subscription retention
Common bottlenecks: Long wait times, manual booking, quality control delays

### Retail
Key processes: Inventory management, Customer service, Staff scheduling
Critical metrics: Stockout rate, Inventory turnover, Shrinkage, NPS
Common bottlenecks: Manual reordering, slow complaint resolution, poor demand forecasting

### Logistics / Transport
Key processes: Order to delivery, Route optimization, Fleet management
Critical metrics: On-time delivery, Cost per delivery, Failed delivery rate
Common bottlenecks: Route inefficiency, failed deliveries, poor tracking communication

### Food / Restaurant
Key processes: Order to table, Supplier procurement, Staff management
Critical metrics: Ticket time, Food cost ratio, Table turnover, Waste rate
Common bottlenecks: Kitchen delays, supplier unreliability, high food waste

### Marketing Agency
Key processes: Campaign delivery, Client reporting, Creative production
Critical metrics: Brief-to-launch time, Client approval rounds, ROAS, Retention rate
Common bottlenecks: Too many revision rounds, slow approvals, late reporting

## How to Use
When a user mentions their sector, apply sector-specific knowledge to:
1. Set appropriate benchmarks
2. Identify the most likely process patterns
3. Focus on industry-specific bottlenecks
4. Recommend sector-appropriate automations
