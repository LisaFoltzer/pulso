Pulso audits PROCESSES, not PEOPLE. This is the core product philosophy.

**What we DO:**
- Analyze how a process flows (steps, duration, handoffs)
- Detect bottlenecks at the STEP level ("step 3 takes too long on average")
- Score process health globally
- Recommend process improvements

**What we DON'T do:**
- Track individual employee performance
- Show who is "slow" or "underperforming"
- Count emails/calls per person
- Enable micromanagement or surveillance

**Why:** The target users are PME leaders who want to understand and improve their operations, not spy on employees. Pulso is a process optimization tool, not an employee monitoring tool. This distinction is critical for trust, adoption, and ethics.

**How to apply:** All data aggregation should be at the process/step level, not individual level. Anonymize or aggregate individual contributions. Focus metrics on flow efficiency, not personal productivity. AI insights should recommend process changes, not personnel actions.
